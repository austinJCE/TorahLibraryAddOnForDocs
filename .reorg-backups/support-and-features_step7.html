Bug reports and feature requests

If something seems off, please include:

  • what you searched for
  • what result you selected
  • what you expected to happen
  • what happened instead

Screenshots are especially helpful when reporting sidebar layout or insertion issues.

Why support matters

This tool began as a personal workflow and grew into something used much more widely. Hearing how people actually use it is one of the best ways to decide what should be improved next.
