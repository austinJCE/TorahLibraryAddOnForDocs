What to include in a bug report

  • which tab you were in: Texts or Voices
  • what you searched for
  • what result you selected
  • whether More Options or Session Settings were involved
  • what you expected to happen
  • what happened instead

Screenshots are especially helpful for sidebar layout, menu positioning, preview mismatches, and insertion issues.
