🎲 Surprise Me
Modified from Legacy beta feature "Popcorn" by Shlomi Helfgot.
🤖 AI Lesson Generator
Generate a structured lesson or source-sheet draft directly in Google Docs. Basic mode uses the Merkaz website default route so most users do not need to choose a provider, model, or API key. Advanced mode keeps provider, model, and request-level key controls for power users.
When enabled, the three AI lesson actions also appear in the add-on’s Quick Actions submenu.
🤖 AI Settings
This pre-fills the AI modal. In Basic mode the site still decides how to route the request.
Only applies when you switch to Advanced mode.
Google may describe the add-on as able to “run when you are not present” because it can make secure external requests to the Merkaz site. In practice, AI requests are only sent when you choose a menu item or click a button in the add-on.
🤖 Saved Keys
Reloads the sidebar from your saved defaults and clears any per-insertion session overrides.
